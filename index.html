FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

I specialize in designing scalable, user-centered platforms for the public sector, governance, and civic innovation. My work spans digital services for government, citizen engagement tools, and operational systems that support large-scale public service delivery. I’ve also contributed to sports tech, education, and nonprofit platforms, with a strong focus on accessibility and impact. 

I lead end-to-end product design—from strategy to execution. My core services include:

  • UX/UI Design for web and mobile platforms
  • Design Systems for scalable, multi-role interfaces
  • Information Architecture & Content Structuring
  • Prototyping & Interaction Design
  • Design Leadership & Mentorship
  • Stakeholder Alignment & Strategic Communication

Absolutely. My portfolio includes:

  • Punjab Jobs: A mobile-first employment platform streamlining job discovery and recruitment workflows
  • Maryam Ki Dastak: A doorstep service delivery system transforming citizen access to public services
  • ESS App: A workplace efficiency tool for PITB employees, improving HR workflows and collaboration
  • City Watch: A smart surveillance dashboard for event security and public safety
  • AQI Platform: A public-facing environmental tool delivering real-time air quality insights across Punjab
  • Each case study is documented with concept, research, design, testing, and impact summaries to showcase process and outcomes.

My process is iterative, impact-driven, and tailored to context. It typically includes:

  1. Discovery – Understanding user needs, system constraints, and institutional goals
  2. Research – Mapping journeys, surfacing pain points, and analyzing usage data
  3. Strategy – Defining design principles, role-based flows, and content priorities
  4. Design – Crafting wireframes, prototypes, and visual systems with accessibility in mind
  5. Feedback & Iteration – Refining through stakeholder input and real-world usage
  6. Delivery – Aligning with dev teams, documenting specs, and supporting implementation 

I use a flexible toolkit depending on the project scope:

  • Figma for interface design, prototyping, and design systems
  • Adobe Illustrator & Photoshop for visual assets and iconography
  • Miro for journey mapping and collaborative workshops
  • Notion & Google Workspace for documentation and stakeholder alignment
  • Jira & Trello for cross-functional coordination
  • Occasionally, Webflow or Framer for interactive mockups or concept demos
